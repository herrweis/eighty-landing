Wordmark

Tagline

The name

eighty° is written lowercase with the degree symbol attached, capital only when it opens a sentence. Always written as eighty°, never spelled out as Eighty Degrees Recovery and Wellness Centre.

The mark

The 80° mark is for signage and physical applications, not a general logo. It always sits border to border on the object it is applied to, filling the surface edge to edge rather than floating within it.

CMYK values are approximate conversions.

Size and space

Keep clear space around the wordmark of at least the height of the “e”. Do not set the wordmark smaller than 16px on screen or 12mm in print. Do not stretch, recolour or add effects.
